New Portal Features & Enhancements

Published By: IT Team

Published: 14th May 2025

We are excited to announce several major updates and new features that have been added to the Staff Portal to make your daily tasks easier and more efficient.

🤖 AI-Powered Chatbot

We've integrated a new staff assistant chatbot! You can find it in the bottom right corner of the portal. It can help you find resources, answer common HR questions, and guide you to the right forms based on your role.

📋 Personal To-Do List

Stay organized with the new built-in To-Do list. Accessible from the sidebar, this feature allows you to keep track of your tasks directly within the portal. Your tasks are saved locally to your browser for quick access.

🔗 Improved Quick Links

The Quick Links section is now role-aware. Admin, HR, and Heads of Department will now see additional relevant links automatically, while keeping the interface clean for all staff.

⚡ Performance & Security

We've optimized the portal's loading times and improved security for password changes. The codebase has also been streamlined for a smoother experience across all devices.

We hope these updates improve your experience with the portal. As always, if you have any feedback or encounter any issues, please let the IT Team know.
